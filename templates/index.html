Driver Drowsiness - Detection System

Welcome to the Drowsiness Detector System. This system helps in detecting drowsiness of drivers to prevent accidents.

Whether it’s due to medication, a sleep disorder or a poor night’s rest, new research points to the risks and potential dangers of drowsy driving. Missing one to two hours of the recommended seven hours of sleep a night nearly doubles the risk of a car accident. Sleepiness can come without warning, so drivers should prioritize getting enough sleep and avoid driving.
